Image Extract

Image Extract is an LLM-powered tool that extracts structured data from images in any industry or function.

It analyzes images to provide detailed, industry-specific information in a user-friendly format.

Start by selecting an industry below. Each has pre-selected image templates demonstrating processable image types and data. Click any template to see the LLM's extraction capabilities.

You can also upload your own image. This app will analyze it and extract relevant information based on the industry context. Results appear in a structured table, which you can review and save as JSON.
